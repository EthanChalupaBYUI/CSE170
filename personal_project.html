Ethan Chalupa | Personal User Manual

What is your Style?

I like working in a small team of people I know. I like knowing my purpose in a team and what the teams purpose is.

When do you like people to approach you and how?

Preferably when I'm not doing something else, but I really dont mind. I like it when people respect me when they talk to me.

How do you like people to communicate with you?

I prefer longer questions face to face, and short questions in a text or email.

How do you make decisions?

I like to think that I think broadly with the consequenses in mind when I make decisions.

How can people help you?

This changes on a per task basis, but I like help and work well in a team when the task requires it.

What will you not tolerate in others?

I severly dislike it when people get in my way due to malicous intent or intentional ignorance. Other than that not much gets to me.
